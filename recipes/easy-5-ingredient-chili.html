Easy 5-Ingredient Chili

Description

My father and I created this easy 5-ingredient chili recipe and everyone loves it. It is fast, simple, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 15 oz tomato sauce
  • 1 can kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can chili beans, not drained
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder, or to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef until browned and crumbled.
  2. Stir in kidney beans, chili beans, and tomato sauce.
  3. Bring to a boil and stir in chili powder. Then reduce heat to simmer, stirring occasionally until thickened, approx. 15 mins.
