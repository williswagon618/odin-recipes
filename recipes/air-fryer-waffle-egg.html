Air Fryer Waffle Egg

Description

Classic egg inside a waffle for a sweet, buttery flavor and added crunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen waffle
  • 1 large egg
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheese
  • maple syrup to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat air fryer to 350 degrees F
  2. Cut a hole in the center of the frozen waffle. Place waffle on parchment paper, then place in air fryer.
  3. Crack egg directly into the center of the waffle hole. Season to taste. Close lid and cook for 5-6 mins until egg white begins to set.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese onto waffle, cook another 1-2 mins until egg white is completely set.
  5. Drizzle with maple syrup and enjoy!
